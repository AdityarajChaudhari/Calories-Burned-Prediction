What is a Calories Burnt Prediction?

This data set consists of various factors that can decide the amount of calories burned by the person during workout.

App Info

The dataset contains various information about persons age, weight, duration he worked out nd various factors that can help us predict the amount of calories burned by the person.

Here we consider features like Age, Weight, Heart Rate to determine the calories burnt by the person after workout.So based on some of these factors one can see the calories burned down by person after working out.

Further we can also analyze the data and then can help us understands how much amount of work one should put to get desired results.
